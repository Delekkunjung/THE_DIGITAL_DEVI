Navadurga - 9 Forms of Goddess Durga

Stories, Mantras, and Videos

1. Shailaputri

Daughter of Himalayas, symbol of strength and devotion.

ॐ देवी शैलपुत्र्यै नमः॥

2. Brahmacharini

Goddess of penance and austerity, embodies wisdom and knowledge.

ॐ देवी ब्रह्मचारिण्यै नमः॥

3. Chandraghanta

Warrior form with bell-shaped moon, protector from demons.

ॐ देवी चन्द्रघण्टायै नमः॥

4. Kushmanda

Creator of the universe, radiates cosmic energy.

ॐ देवी कूष्माण्डायै नमः॥

5. Skandamata

Mother of Skanda (Kartikeya), grants wisdom and salvation.

ॐ देवी स्कन्दमातायै नमः॥

6. Katyayani

Fierce warrior form born to sage Katyayan, destroys evil.

ॐ देवी कात्यायन्यै नमः॥

7. Kalaratri

Dark form, remover of fear and negativity.

ॐ देवी कालरात्र्यै नमः॥

8. Mahagauri

Pure, serene form symbolizing peace and purity.

ॐ देवी महागौर्यै नमः॥

9. Siddhidatri

Bestower of siddhis (supernatural powers) and blessings.

ॐ देवी सिद्धिदात्र्यै नमः॥
